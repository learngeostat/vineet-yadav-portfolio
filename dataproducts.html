Data Products

Operational, bias-corrected, gridded datasets hosted at NASA GES DISC. Each card links directly to the DOI record.

OCO-2 Gridded XCO₂ (Level 4)
Daily global, bias-corrected XCO₂ & select fields

Daily, gridded fields of bias-corrected column-averaged CO₂ (XCO₂) aggregated from OCO-2 L2 retrievals for analysis-ready use. Includes consistent quality screening and ancillary variables for inversions and climate analysis. Archived at NASA GES DISC with a citable DOI for reproducible workflows.

Multi-Instrument Fused XCO₂ (Level 4)
Daily global, bias-corrected, fused coverage

Daily fused XCO₂ that combines OCO-2 with other satellites (e.g., GOSAT) under a unified bias-correction framework. Fusion substantially improves spatiotemporal coverage relative to single-sensor products. Archived at GES DISC with full metadata for traceability and tooling interoperability.

Level 4 Cadence: Daily Vars: XCO₂ (+ anc.)
Access data (DOI) Docs/CMR
OCO-2 Gridded XCO₂ + SIF (Level 3)
Daily global XCO₂ plus SIF with UQ

Daily, gridded OCO-2 XCO₂ alongside solar-induced fluorescence (SIF) to support biosphere–atmosphere carbon studies. Provides SIF with quantified uncertainties using a Bayesian hierarchical modeling approach on a uniform grid. Curated at GES DISC with a persistent DOI for citation and automated access.

Level 3 Cadence: Daily Vars: XCO₂, SIF
Access data (DOI) Docs/CMR
Level 3 = gridded/derived; Level 4 = model- or analysis-ready (e.g., fused/assimilated).
